CAN YOU DELETE OLD DEPLOYMENTS?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Technically: Yes, but it's not necessary
Practically: Leave them alone

Why keep them?
  ✓ Rollback capability - if current deployment breaks, you can go back
  ✓ History - see what changed between deployments
  ✓ Debugging - check logs from previous deployments
  ✓ No harm - they don't affect your live site

Why delete them?
  ✗ Saves minimal disk space
  ✗ Cleaner dashboard (cosmetic only)

Recommendation: Leave them. They're useful for debugging.

HOW TO DELETE OLD DEPLOYMENTS (IF YOU WANT)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Go to: https://dashboard.render.com
2. Click on your service (frontend or backend)
3. Go to "Deployments" tab
4. Find the old deployment you want to delete
5. Click the three dots (⋯) menu
6. Select "Delete"
7. Confirm

⚠️ WARNING: Only delete old deployments, NOT the current one!
