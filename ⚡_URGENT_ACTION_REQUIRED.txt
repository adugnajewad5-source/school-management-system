WHAT'S BEING FIXED
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

File: backend/index.js (line 30)

BEFORE (doesn't work with PlanetScale):
  const db = mysql.createConnection({
    host: process.env.DB_HOST || 'localhost',
    user: process.env.DB_USER || 'root',
    password: process.env.DB_PASSWORD || '',
    database: process.env.DB_NAME || 'school_management'
  });

AFTER (works with PlanetScale):
  const db = mysql.createConnection({
    host: process.env.DB_HOST || 'localhost',
    user: process.env.DB_USER || 'root',
    password: process.env.DB_PASSWORD || '',
    database: process.env.DB_NAME || 'school_management',
    ssl: 'Amazon RDS',              ← THIS IS THE FIX
    waitForConnections: true,
    connectionLimit: 10,
    queueLimit: 0
  });

The ssl: 'Amazon RDS' tells mysql2 to use SSL/TLS encryption, which 
PlanetScale requires.
