## 📝 **LOGIN INSTRUCTIONS**

### For **REGISTERED STUDENTS**:
1. Go to login page
2. Enter your **username** (e.g., `mame@12`) OR **email** (e.g., `mame@gmail.com`)
3. Enter your **chosen password** (the one you created during registration)
4. Click Login

### For **UNREGISTERED STUDENTS**:
1. Go to login page
2. Enter your **Student ID** (e.g., `STU-357`)
3. Enter your **temporary password** (e.g., `Adug@357`)
4. Click Login
5. **First time login**: You'll be prompted to complete registration with email and new password
